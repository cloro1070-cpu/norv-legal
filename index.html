Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the NORV mobile application (“NORV”, “the app”, “we”, “us”) handles information. NORV is designed to be private by default: the app works entirely offline and does not require an account.

01Who we are

NORV is created and operated by Charlène Dumas, an independent developer, who acts as the data controller for the app (“we”, “us”).

For any question regarding this policy or your privacy, you can contact us at contact@studiosharl.com.

02Our approach to your data

NORV is built to keep your information on your device. We do not operate user accounts, we do not run a backend server that stores your personal content, and we do not sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

All the data you create in the app — your habits, streaks, progress, scores, and settings — is stored locally on your device using your device’s standard storage. This information never leaves your phone through us, and we have no access to it.

If you delete the app, this locally stored data is removed along with it.

03Crash and error reporting (Sentry)

To keep NORV stable and fix problems quickly, the app uses Sentry, a third-party error-monitoring service, to collect technical diagnostic information when the app crashes or encounters an error.

What is collected:

  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Technical performance and session diagnostic data needed to reproduce and fix the issue
  • Limited session replay information that is triggered when an error occurs

Privacy protections applied:

  • Sentry data is hosted on servers located in the European Union.
  • Masking is enabled: text, images, and webview content are masked in session replay data, so the content of what you type or view is not exposed in these diagnostic reports.
  • This data is technical in nature. It is used only to diagnose and fix problems, is not linked to your identity, and is not used to track you across other apps or services.

For more information on how Sentry processes data, see Sentry’s privacy policy.

04What we do not do

To be clear, NORV does not:

  • require you to create an account or provide an email, name, or password to use the app
  • include third-party advertising
  • include third-party analytics or marketing trackers
  • track you across other apps, websites, or services
  • collect your location, contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • sell or rent any data to third parties

05Legal basis and your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the processing of crash and diagnostic data described above is based on our legitimate interest in maintaining a stable, functioning, and secure application.

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have rights including the right to access, rectify, or request the erasure of personal data, the right to restrict or object to processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Because the diagnostic data collected through Sentry is technical and not linked to your identity, we are generally unable to associate it with a specific individual. If you wish to exercise any of your rights or have questions, please contact us at contact@studiosharl.com and we will respond in accordance with applicable law.

06Children’s privacy

NORV is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

07Data retention

Diagnostic and crash data collected through Sentry is retained only for as long as necessary to investigate and resolve technical issues, in accordance with Sentry’s standard retention practices.

The personal content you create in the app is retained on your device for as long as you keep the app installed, and is under your control.

08Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be reflected in an updated version of the app where appropriate.
